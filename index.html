About Me

Prof. Nedu is a Historian, an Educator, a Full Stack Developer (in view), an Art Enthusiast, a believer of humanity and God. He was born in Lagos Nigeria in the late 80s, He lived most of his earlychildhood and teens in Lagos, where he got a formal primary education in a private school, and further went on to get a Secondary education in a Military school in Lagos still. After his secondary education, he moved on to the eastern region of Nigeria, precisely Anambra State of Nigeria to get a formal tertiary education. He graduated with honours in the year 2019, He worked as a Liberian and a Curator during his formal education in the eastern region, and also taught History and Mathematics during his time in the eastern region till the hit of Covid-19 and the lapses and flaw in the educational sector, gave him the boost to relocate back to Lagos, to begin a career in an Art Gallery. He is currently based in Lagos, freelancing in the creativity space and also still carrying out academic research and delivering academic documentations periodically. And also studying to become a renowned Software engineering to be able to make innovative impacts in the educational sector and globally.

He works with Blue Sky Arts & Studio Festac, Lagos as an Administrative Officer, his role as an administrative officer has led him, to delivering clerical support by handling a range of routine and special requirements for the establishment. And His role as an administrative officer has been commendable over the years.

I'm a passionate developer with expertise in web technologies.
